## Overview

A combination of button styles can be used to signal actions, communicate hierarchy and relationship, and support procedural knowledge. Although there are some guidelines, it is encouraged to determine patterns that work best in the specific context of individual tasks or screens and applications. Buttons can be labeled with text, icons, or a combination of icons and text.
